Hi, I'm Keith Buhler.

I'm a philosopher, educator, researcher, and writer.

I started teaching the "great books" about 15 years ago. Since then, I have had the privilege of teaching college, high school students, and adults. I currently guide students in philosophy and ethics at the University of Kentucky and Asbury University. Folks tend to really like my classes.

My dissertation research examines the concepts of virtue and practical reason in Alasdair MacIntre, Philippa Foot, John McDowell, and Robert Adams. I'm also interested in metaphysics, philosophy of mind and imagination, and philosophy of religion..

When time permits, I write fiction. I help others to read intentionally.

This website is the start of a bigger project. In the meantime, enjoy.

Keith
