Hi, I'm Keith Buhler.

[](Keith)

I'm a philosophy researcher, educator, writer, and speaker.

I teach philosophy at the [University of Kentucky](https://philosophy.as.uky.edu/users/kebu226) and Asbury University. And I'm writing a dissertation about the concept of virtue in Alasdair MacIntre, Philippa Foot, Rosalind Hursthouse, John McDowell, and Robert Adams.

I'm also interested in Plato, philosophy of mind, and philosophy of religion.

You can check out my [student reviews](http://www.ratemyprofessors.com/ShowRatings.jsp?tid=1822771), [read some academic papers](https://uky.academia.edu/KeithBuhler), check out my blog on [reading intentionally](http://www.readingintentionally.com/), or my [book](http://www.amazon.com/Sola-Scriptura-Dialogue-Keith-Buhler/dp/1475270860/ref=sr_1_11?ie=UTF8&qid=1351488005&sr=8-11&keywords=sola+scriptura).

This website is the start of a bigger project. In the meantime, enjoy. Keith (keithedbuhler at gmail dot com) @keith_buhler
