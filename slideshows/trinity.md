### Justin Martyr

![justin](http://www.conservapedia.com/File:Justin_Martyr.jpg)

- Greek philosopher
- Converted 132 AD
- Wrote about the union of faith and philosophy
- Wrote to Jews, fellow Greeks, and Roman emporers
- Died 165 AD, as a martyr
